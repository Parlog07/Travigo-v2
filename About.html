Who Are We

Founded in 2005 by three university friends in Düsseldorf (Germany), trivago
since become a leading global accommodation search website. We are
focused on reshaping the way millions of travelers search for and compare
hotels and other accommodations. Part of Expedia Group (NASDAQ: TRVG),
trivago’s mission is to become your companion to experience our world.
